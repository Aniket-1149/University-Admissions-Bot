MASTER OF SCIENCE IN COMPUTER SCIENCE

Program Details:
- Duration: 2 years (full-time)
- Degree: Master of Science (M.S.)
- Start Date: September 2026

Stanford has been ranked #1 in Computer Science for 15 consecutive years! Our beautiful campus spans 8,180 acres in the heart of Silicon Valley. Join thousands of successful alumni who have gone on to change the world!

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS:

Academic Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field from an accredited institution
- Minimum GPA: 3.5/4.0 (undergraduate cumulative)
- Strong background in mathematics, algorithms, and programming
- Relevant coursework in data structures, operating systems, and computer architecture

Our faculty are world-renowned experts! We have state-of-the-art research facilities and partnerships with leading tech companies.

Standardized Test Scores:
- GRE General Test required (minimum scores: Verbal 155, Quantitative 165, Analytical Writing 4.0)
- TOEFL (for international students): Minimum 100 iBT
- IELTS (alternative): Minimum overall band score 7.0

Required Documents:
- Official transcripts from all universities attended
- Three letters of recommendation (academic or professional)
- Statement of Purpose (maximum 1000 words)
- Current resume/CV
- Writing sample or research paper (optional but recommended)

IMPORTANT DEADLINES:

Application Deadline: December 15, 2025
Financial Aid Application: December 15, 2025
Admission Decision Notification: March 15, 2026
Enrollment Confirmation: April 15, 2026

Stanford's campus life is incredible! We offer 650+ student organizations, Division I athletics, and countless opportunities for personal growth.

FINANCIAL INFORMATION:

Application Fee: $125 (non-refundable)
Tuition Fee: $58,416 per year
Estimated Total Cost (including living expenses): $85,000 per year

Financial aid is available! 80% of our graduate students receive some form of funding. Research assistantships, teaching assistantships, and fellowships are competitive but widely available.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION:

- Interviews are conducted on an invitation-only basis
- Part-time enrollment is not available for this program
- Transfer credits: Maximum 45 quarter units may be transferred from other institutions
- English language proficiency required for all teaching assistantships

Experience the Stanford difference! Our collaborative environment fosters innovation and excellence. Don't miss this opportunity to study alongside the brightest minds in technology!

MASTER OF BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION (MBA)

Program Details:
- Duration: 2 years (full-time)
- Degree: Master of Business Administration
- Start Date: September 2026

Join Stanford's legendary MBA program where innovation meets entrepreneurship!

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS:

Academic Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution (any field)
- Minimum GPA: 3.3/4.0
- Average work experience: 4-5 years (recommended but not required)

Test Scores:
- GMAT or GRE required
- GMAT: Average admitted score 738
- GRE: Average Verbal 164, Quantitative 165
- TOEFL: Minimum 100 iBT (for non-native English speakers)

Required Documents:
- Official transcripts
- Two letters of recommendation (professional preferred)
- Resume/CV
- Essays (multiple prompts provided in application)
- Video introduction

APPLICATION DEADLINES:

Round 1: September 15, 2025
Round 2: January 10, 2026
Round 3: April 5, 2026 (limited spots available)

FEES:

Application Fee: $275
Tuition: $76,950 per year
